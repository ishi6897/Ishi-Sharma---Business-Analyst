# Retail Sales & Customer Order Management System

## Domain
Retail & E-Commerce

## Overview
A retail sales platform designed to manage customer orders, shopping baskets, discounts, loyalty vouchers, payments, and order fulfillment. The system enables retail staff to process customer purchases efficiently while supporting multiple payment methods and promotional offers.

## Business Problem
Retail stores often face challenges in managing customer transactions, discounts, loyalty programs, and payment processing across multiple sales channels. This system centralizes the sales process and improves transaction accuracy.
