# Equipment Rental Management System

## Domain
Equipment Rental & Asset Management

## Overview
A web-based platform that enables customers to search, compare, rent, and manage equipment online. The system provides equipment discovery, rental pricing calculation, cart management, booking confirmation, and payment processing functionalities to streamline the equipment rental process.

## Business Problem
Traditional equipment rental processes often involve manual inventory checks, phone-based bookings, and limited visibility into equipment availability. This system digitizes the rental journey, improving customer experience and operational efficiency.
